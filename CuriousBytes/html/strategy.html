My Strategy

How I studied smart and cracked GATE CS with AIR 2:

  • Understand the syllabus deeply and break it down by priority
  • Maintain short notes for every subject
  • Focus more on problem-solving than just theory
  • Join a good test series and analyze every test
  • Keep revising every week
